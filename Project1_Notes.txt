Project 1: Business Performance KPI Dashboard
Project Timeline & Daily Breakdown

Day 1 – Project Setup & Data Understanding

* Focus of the day: Project planning, data familiarisation, and KPI definition.

* What I did:

- Defined the business objective: analysing overall business performance using key metrics.

Reviewed the dataset to understand available fields (orders, sales, profit, quantity, dates, regions, and categories).

* Identified core KPIs required for decision-making:

-Total Orders

- Total Quantity

- Total Sales

- Total Profit

* Created the project folder structure and prepared files for analysis.

* Outcome:

- Clear understanding of the dataset.

- KPIs aligned with business performance goals.

- Project scope and dashboard structure defined.




Day 2 – Data Cleaning & Transformation

* Focus of the day: Preparing clean, analysis-ready data.

* What I did:

- Imported the dataset into Power BI.

* Cleaned and transformed the data using Power Query Editor:

- Checked data types and corrected inconsistencies.

- Removed duplicates and irrelevant columns.

- Ensured date fields were properly formatted for time-based analysis.

- Validated data accuracy before analysis.

* Outcome:

Clean, structured dataset ready for KPI calculations.

Reliable foundation for analysis and visualisation.




Day 3 – KPI Creation & Analysis

* Focus of the day: KPI calculation and analytical insights.

* What I did:

* Created calculated measures for:

- Total Orders

- Total Quantity

- Total Sales

- Total Profit

* Analysed trends across:

- Time (yearly and monthly performance)

- Product categories

- Regions

- Identified key patterns such as top-performing regions and categories.

* Outcome:

- Accurate KPIs reflecting business performance.

- Analytical insights to support strategic decision-making.




Day 4 – Dashboard Design, Insights & Recommendations

* Focus of the day: Visual storytelling and business value delivery.

* What I did:

- Designed an interactive Business Performance KPI Dashboard in Power BI.

* Added visuals including:

- KPI cards

- Line charts for trends

- Bar charts for regional and category performance

- Slicers for user interaction

- Wrote a structured Insights & Recommendations document explaining findings in business terms.

- Reviewed and polished the dashboard for clarity and presentation.

* Outcome:

- Final, professional Power BI dashboard.

- Clear insights highlighting performance strengths and improvement opportunities.

- Business-ready recommendations suitable for stakeholders.

* Project Completion

* This project demonstrates:

- End-to-end business intelligence workflow

- KPI design and analysis

- Data cleaning and transformation

- Interactive dashboard development

- Business-focused insights and recommendations
